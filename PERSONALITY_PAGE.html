Impact of Traits and Emotions

One of my strongest traits is determination. This trait has a significant impact on my academic life:

  • Focused Goals: Determination helps me set and achieve specific academic goals.

  • Overcoming Challenges: It drives me to persist through difficult subjects and assignments.

  • Consistent Effort: My determination ensures that I consistently put in effort and time.

Favourite Quote and Person

My favorite person is my Lord and Savior, Jesus. He is always there for me, even though it might not be physically but He finds ways to communicate with me and show me that He is present whenever I need help, no matter what the situation is.

My favourite quotes are 2 bible verses which states:

"I can do all this through Christ who gives me strength." – Philippians 4:13
"With man this is impossible, but with God all things are possible. " – Matthew 19:26

Those verses help me realize that, even when a task feels impossible, I can rely on my higher power to guide me in completing it.
